## The problem you already know

When a hail event crosses central Florida, four things happen — and your reps usually arrive fourth.

1. **The storm passes**, leaving 4,000–6,000 residential roofs damaged in its footprint.
2. **Out-of-state storm chasers** roll trucks across state lines within 48 hours. Their entire business model is being there first.
3. **National lead-gen brokers** ($50–$200/lead) light up Google with hail-damage ads in the affected ZIPs by morning.
4. **Your reps** find out about it from the news, scramble a canvass plan by mid-week, and start knocking doors *after* the easy conversions have been booked.

You're not losing the deals because of price. You're losing them because someone else got the door first.
