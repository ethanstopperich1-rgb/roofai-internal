How accurate is the number? +

The estimate is a satellite-measured starting point — typically within a 10–15% band of an on-site quote on standard residential roofs, wider on complex or large properties. A binding contract still requires a master roofer on the property.

Is this truly free? +

Yes. The estimate is complimentary. There is no fee, deposit, or obligation, and you are never charged for the on-site visit if you choose to request one.

Will I be spammed or cold-called? +

No. Your details are never sold, never syndicated. No one follows up unless you ask, and a single reply of STOP ends contact immediately.
